Millco Organic & Fresh Food Products is a Kerala-based manufacturing facility specializing in controlled coconut processing and export-grade edible oil production. We source fresh coconuts directly and process them in-house using regulated electric drying systems to ensure consistent copra quality without sulphur treatment.

From Fresh Coconut to Export Container — Fully Controlled, Fully Documented

01

Fresh Coconut Sourcing

We procure exclusively from verified fresh coconut sources. No market copra is purchased or used at any stage of our manufacturing process.

02

Controlled Electric Drying

Copra transformation is carried out using regulated electric drying systems with precisely controlled temperature, humidity, and time parameters to ensure uniformity and quality.

03

Zero Chemical Treatment

No sulphur or chemical additives are introduced at any point. Our zero-sulphur policy is maintained as a core operational standard, not an optional feature.

04

Mechanical Oil Extraction

Oil is extracted through mechanical cold-press processes, preserving the natural composition of the coconut oil and ensuring compliance with export-grade specifications.

05

Batch Monitoring & Testing

Every production batch is monitored and tested against defined quality parameters. Records are maintained for full traceability and regulatory compliance.

06

Warehouse Storage & Export Dispatch

Finished goods are warehoused in controlled conditions and dispatched for export with complete documentation including COA, COO, phytosanitary certificates, and shipping documentation.
