Quality Insights

Why Sulphur-Free Processing Matters

Sulphur dioxide in conventional copra drying leaves chemical residues that compromise oil purity, flavour, and chemical parameters. Millco's zero-sulphur process delivers measurably cleaner oil.

Lab-Verified Purity

Every production batch is tested against defined quality parameters including FFA, moisture, and peroxide value. Our sulphur-free process consistently delivers lower acid values and superior shelf stability compared to sulphur-treated equivalents.

Consistent Export Data

With documented batch records, traceability from coconut to container, and APEDA endorsements, our export shipments meet the stringent import requirements of GCC, African, and Central Asian buyers without rejections or deviations.

In-Depth Process Control

Temperature, humidity, and drying duration are monitored continuously. No open-air sun drying, no market copra purchase, no chemical treatment — just precise, repeatable processing from fresh coconut to refined bulk oil.

Our Facility

Manufacturing Infrastructure

Fully integrated facility in Malappuram, Kerala — from fresh coconut intake to export-ready container loading.

01

Fresh Coconut Intake

Coconuts sourced directly from verified growers. Intake quality checked before copra transformation begins. No market copra is used.

02

Controlled Electric Drying

Copra transformation via regulated electric drying systems with monitored temperature and humidity. Zero sulphur, zero open-air exposure.

03

Mechanical Oil Extraction

Cold-press mechanical extraction preserving the natural profile of the oil. Suitable for food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic end uses.

04

Quality Testing Lab

In-house and third-party batch testing for FFA, moisture, peroxide value, and other key parameters. Full batch records maintained.

05

Warehousing & Dispatch

Finished goods stored in controlled conditions. Export dispatch with complete documentation: COA, COO, phytosanitary, APEDA endorsement.

06

Container Loading

FCL operations for high-volume buyers. LCL consolidation available for trial orders and smaller consignments through major Indian ports.
